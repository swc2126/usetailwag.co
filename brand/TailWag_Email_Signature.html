How to set this as your Gmail signature

1
Click the Copy Signature button below
2
Go to Gmail → Settings (gear icon) → See all settings → General
3
Scroll to Signature, click Create new, name it TailWag, click Create
4
Click inside the signature editor box and press Ctrl+A then Ctrl+V (or ⌘A / ⌘V on Mac) to paste
5
Scroll down and click Save Changes
