2. Description of Service

TailWag provides a cloud-based SMS communication platform designed for independent dog daycares, boarding facilities, grooming salons, and training centers. The Service enables Subscribers to send personalized text messages to their clients ("Pet Parents") on behalf of the Subscriber's business.

Core features include:

  • Personalized Report Cards — AI-assisted messages sent to Pet Parents summarizing their pet's visit, generated from staff-entered notes
  • Appointment Reminders — Automated SMS reminders to reduce no-shows, configured by the Subscriber
  • Review Requests — Timed requests for Google reviews, sent to Pet Parents after positive visits
  • Dedicated Phone Number — A local phone number provisioned for the Subscriber's outbound messaging
  • Sentiment Monitoring — Automated analysis of staff notes to flag concerning visits and route positive experiences toward review requests
  • Media Messaging — Ability to include photos in SMS/MMS messages (plan-dependent)

Specific features and messaging limits vary by subscription plan. TailWag reserves the right to modify, add, or discontinue features with reasonable notice to Subscribers.
