Go to Directory, search for a client, and click their name to open their profile. From there, select a template or write a custom message, optionally add a photo, and hit Send. The message is delivered as an SMS to the pet parent's phone number on file.
Yes — replies come into your TailWag inbox and are visible to your whole team. You'll see an unread indicator on the messaging page. Each client has their own thread.
After sending a report card, you can optionally follow up with a review request SMS that includes your Google review link. Set your Google review URL in Settings and TailWag will handle the timing and message formatting automatically.
Go to Team and click "Invite Team Member." Enter their email address and assign a role — Manager or Team Member. They'll receive an email invite to create their account and will have access to your daycare's clients.
Download the import template, fill in your client and dog information, and upload it on the Import page. Coming from Gingr? Use the Gingr import tool to automatically map your existing data.
Message limits depend on your plan. Check your current usage and plan details in Billing & Plan. If you're running into limits, upgrading your plan takes effect immediately.
Go to Settings and click Manage Subscription under Your Plan. This opens the Stripe billing portal where you can upgrade, downgrade, update your payment method, or cancel — all without contacting support. If you don't see the button, and we'll help right away.
TailWag is a web app that works great on mobile browsers — just visit usetailwag.co on your phone and add it to your home screen for a near-native experience. A dedicated iOS and Android app is on the roadmap.
