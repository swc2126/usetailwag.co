About TailWag

The tail wags the dog.

There's an old expression — the tail wagging the dog — used to describe when something small drives something much bigger than itself. At TailWag, we took that idea and made it our mission. A single text message sent at the right moment — a report card that makes a pet parent smile on their lunch break, a reminder that prevents a missed appointment, a review request that arrives just as a client is still glowing from pickup — these are small things. But small things, done consistently and done well, drive enormous outcomes. More trust. More loyalty. More five-star reviews. More revenue. The tail wags the dog. Good communication creates happy clients. Happy clients create a thriving business. And a thriving business creates the freedom that every independent daycare owner started working toward in the first place.

A wagging tail is the most honest signal in the world. Dogs don't perform happiness — they just feel it, and their whole body shows it.

That's the standard we hold every message TailWag sends to. Not automated. Not generic. Not something that sounds like software wrote it. Something that sounds like the person who loves that dog wrote it — because in a way, they did. The staff member who spent the day with Buddy knows he was playful and ate great and finally made a friend in the yard. TailWag just makes sure his family hears about it before dinner.

When pet parents receive a message like that, something happens. They feel it. They trust you more. They tell their friends. They stay. That's a wagging tail. And that's what we're here to create — for every dog, every visit, every day.
