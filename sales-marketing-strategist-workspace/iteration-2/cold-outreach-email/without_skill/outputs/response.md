## Email Template

---

**Subject Line:** Quick question about your doggy daycare

---

**Here's what I'm building:** TailWag is an AI-powered SMS platform that automatically generates personalized report cards for every dog at your daycare. You handle the operations, and TailWag handles the customer communication—appointment reminders, photo updates, Google review requests, the whole workflow.

The result? Pet parents get the personalized touch they love, you get back 5-10 hours every week, and your review ratings go up because pet parents actually read the personalized messages.

I'm not asking you to sign up for anything yet—I'm still building this out based on feedback from owners like you. But I'd love to chat for 15 minutes and see if this is something that would actually solve a real problem at your facility.

Are you open to a quick call next week?

[Your name]

## Implementation Tips

### Finding Email Addresses
- Use LinkedIn to search for daycare owners and find email addresses
- Google Maps: Search "dog daycare [city]" → click the business profile → often has owner name and contact info
- ZoomInfo or Hunter.io can help find business emails
- Facebook business pages sometimes list contact info

### Personalization Checklist
- Replace [Owner Name] with actual name (LinkedIn or Google Maps)
- Replace [Daycare Name] with the actual business name
- Replace [City/Area] with their location
- Add a specific detail you noticed about their facility if possible (e.g., "Saw you mention your 2-acre play area" or "Love how interactive your Instagram is")

### Send Strategy
- Send Tue-Thu, 9am-2pm (higher open rates)
- Don't mass-email at once—stagger over 2-3 weeks to stay personalized
- Follow up once after 5-7 days if no response
- A/B test subject lines with owners you know to see what works best

### Key Messaging Pillars (if they ask for more detail)
1. **Time savings:** Automates the time-intensive reporting task
2. **Customer satisfaction:** Pet parents get the personalization they pay for
3. **Growth:** Better reviews, word-of-mouth referrals, competitive advantage
4. **Ease of use:** Minimal lift on their end—works within existing operations

## Customization Notes

Feel free to adjust the tone based on your style:
- **Slightly more playful:** "I may have obsessed over your daycare's Instagram" (if they're active there)
- **More data-driven:** Lead with a stat about pet parent expectations
- **More solution-focused:** Lead with "I've built a tool that saves daycare owners 5-10 hours weekly"

The goal is to **spark curiosity, demonstrate understanding of their pain, and make it easy to say yes to a 15-minute conversation.**

Good luck with outreach!
